About Me

My name is Kerri Sexton and I am crrently a Web development student at UNC Chapel Hill's Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp. A little background on me, I graduated with a B.S. degree in Computer Science 2017. Due to scheduling conflicts, I didn't get to take any web development classes while in school. I came across UNC's bootcamp and it seemed like the perfect opportunity to learn more, as well as expand my career opportunities. For leisurely activities, I enjoy: working out, hiking, running, and playing video games.
